Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata was born in Bombay, now Mumbai, during the British Raj, into a Parsi Zoroastrian family, on 28 December 1937.

India’s economy has witnessed significant growth and development over the years, with several influential figures playing a crucial role in shaping its trajectory. In the history of India’s economy, there shines a luminary figure whose success story has become a source of inspiration for generations- Ratan Tata!

Ratan Tata received the Padma Bhushan in 2000 and Padma Vibhushan in 2008,the third and second highest civilian honours awarded by the Government of India

He has recently received awards such as:

  1. Honorary Officer of the Order of Australia (AO) by King Charles III. in 2023
  2. Honorary Doctorate of Literature by HSNC University in 2022.

There are many more awards and achievement which can be found on Wikipedia
